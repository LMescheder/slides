## The Function Space Operator
What's better than a new idea is an operator
that turns old ideas into new ideas

Max Welling (paraphrased)

The Function Space Operator: $$f: \mathcal{Y} \to \mathcal{V}^\mathcal{S} \Rightarrow FS(f): \mathcal{Y} \times \mathcal{S} \to \mathcal{V}$$
Example:
Occupancy Network:
$$FS\left(\mathcal{Y} \to \mathbb [0, 1]^{\mathbb R^3}\right)$$
