## Generative Adversarial Networks

Optimization objective:

$$ \mathcal{L}( \color{blue}{\theta}, \color{red}{\psi} ) = \mathrm{E}_{ \color{blue}{x_1 \sim p_\theta} } \left[ \color{brown}{\varphi_1} ( \color{red}{D_\psi}(\color{blue}{x_1})) \right] + \mathrm{E}_{ \color{olive}{x_2 \sim p_\mathcal{D}}} \left[ \color{brown}{\varphi_2}( -\color{red}{D_\psi}(\color{olive}{x_2})) \right] $$
Symbol Meaning
$\color{olive}{p_\mathcal{D}}$ True data distribution
$\color{blue}{p_\theta}$ Generator distribution
$\color{red}{D_\psi}$ Discriminator
$\color{brown}{\varphi_1}$, $\color{brown}{\varphi_2}$ Activation functions (e.g. softplus)
